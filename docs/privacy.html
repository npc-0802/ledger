Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Palate Map is a personal taste intelligence platform. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We keep it simple because we keep it minimal.

What we collect

  • Account information: your display name and email address, provided when you sign in via Google or magic link.
  • Taste data: your film ratings, scoring weights, archetype results, and preferences — the data you explicitly create.
  • Auth data: session tokens managed by Supabase, our backend provider. We do not store passwords.

We do not collect analytics, tracking pixels, or behavioral data beyond what you directly enter into the app.

How we use it

  • To sync your data across devices so you can pick up where you left off.
  • To derive your palate type from your quiz answers and scoring patterns.
  • To generate AI-powered film predictions via Claude (Anthropic), using only your taste data — no personally identifiable information is sent.

We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — database and authentication. Data is stored in US-based servers. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Google OAuth — optional sign-in. We receive only your name and email. Google Privacy Policy.
  • TMDB (The Movie Database) — film metadata and poster images. No personal data is shared.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI predictions. Only anonymized taste fingerprints are sent, not your name or email.

Data retention & deletion

Your data is stored as long as your account exists. You can sign out and request deletion at any time by emailing us. We will delete your profile and all associated data within 30 days.

Your rights

You can export your film data at any time from the app (Profile → Export). You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold.
